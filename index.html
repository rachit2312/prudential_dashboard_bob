02. The Human + Agent Operating Model

A practical model for how work is delegated, supervised, governed, and continuously improved.

Human + Agent Work System

People bring

  • Judgment and empathy
  • Creativity and problem solving
  • Client and stakeholder relationships
  • Accountability for decisions

Agents bring

  • Scale, speed, and consistency
  • Data processing and synthesis
  • Workflow automation
  • 24/7 execution support
The goal is not to replace people. It is to elevate human impact by assigning the right work to humans, agents, or both.

Operating Model Elements

Executive AI CouncilStrategy, investment, enterprise alignment.
AI Transformation OfficeStandards, governance, enablement, measurement.
Business AI PodsCross-functional teams redesigning priority workflows.
AI Champions NetworkPeer learning, adoption support, best-practice sharing.
Platform & Risk GovernanceSecurity, compliance, responsible AI, model risk.

Design Principles

Human centered
Humans remain accountable for judgment, decisions, and relationships.
Responsible by design
Risk, privacy, compliance, and ethics embedded from the start.
Augment, don’t automate blindly
Agents free capacity for higher-value work.
Embedded in workflows
AI appears where work happens, not in disconnected tools.
Continuous learning
Telemetry and feedback improve people, processes, and agents.

What Drives Adoption?

  • Clear value: solves real problems and saves time.

04. 12-Month Workforce Transformation Roadmap

From foundation to scale: redesign work, build capability, and deliver measurable value through human + agent collaboration.

Months 1–3
Foundation
  • Define vision and principles.
  • Establish governance and cadence.
  • Assess roles, skills, processes, tools, and culture.
  • Prioritize high-value use cases.
  • Define metrics and value targets.
Months 4–6
Design & Align
  • Design human + agent operating model.
  • Define new roles: agent supervisors, product owners, quality governors.
  • Create redesign playbooks and localization guidance.
  • Stand up Innovation Academy and champions network.
  • Create change and communication plan.
Months 7–9
Build & Pilot
  • Launch priority pilots across functions and geographies.
  • Implement upskilling and manager enablement.
  • Deploy agents and workflow changes with governance.
  • Measure adoption, productivity, experience, and risk.
  • Refine operating model.
Months 10–12
Scale & Optimize
  • Scale proven models enterprise-wide.
  • Embed human + agent ways of working.
  • Optimize roles, processes, and talent deployment.
  • Expand autonomous workflows safely.
  • Track and communicate enterprise value.
